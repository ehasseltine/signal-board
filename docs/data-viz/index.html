239 sources read every day. Signal Board synthesizes news coverage across national, international, and community outlets to help you see how different parts of the world and different parts of America are experiencing the same events differently.

Our process:

  • We monitor 239 news sources daily, including national bureaus, international outlets, and community news organizations
  • We classify articles into 10 domains: Governance, Security, Geopolitics, Economics, Politics, Legal, Information, Climate, Labor, and AI
  • We identify recurring patterns and connections across these domains
  • We synthesize the day's reporting into five "mega-stories" — connections that span multiple domains and outlet types
  • We extract how different outlet types (national, international, community) frame each story differently
  • This synthesis is updated daily at 4 PM ET

Why this matters: Most news apps show you one perspective. Signal Board shows you three, so you can see which details national outlets skip, which international angles American news misses, and which local impacts are invisible to national coverage.
